College Experience

My college experience has been surreal because I meet different kinds of people every day. I have classmates who come from various backgrounds, and each one has a unique story. Despite the diversity, I have found a group of friends I bond with regularly.

I know that in college, nothing is permanent. Even the friends you see every day may eventually leave. Some may become irregular students, while others may fail and stop studying. That reality makes me value my current friendships even more.

At the same time, I feel lost in my chosen course. This is not my dream course, and I cannot yet see myself as an IT professional. I sometimes question whether I am on the right path. However, I do not allow these doubts to stop me.

I remind myself that my loved ones are supporting my education. Because of their sacrifices, I continue to study with determination. I grab every opportunity to learn new skills related to my course. My college life is a journey of self-discovery and a test of patience and responsibility.
